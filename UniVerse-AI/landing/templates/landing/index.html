About UniVerse AI

UniVerse AI is designed to enhance the academic experience for Leiden University students. This AI-powered virtual assistant offers personalized support by managing schedules, answering questions, and providing instant help for academic and university-related tasks. By integrating intelligent features such as chatbots and calendar synchronization, UniVerse AI aims to simplify your day-to-day university life and help you stay organized, focused, and productive.
